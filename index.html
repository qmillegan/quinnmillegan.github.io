XV: Oceans (Exploratory Ventures)

March 2013 – June 2013

Exploratory Ventures is a class taught at Riverdale High School by high school students. This project-based course allows students to learn a wide variety of skills and immediately apply them.

XV: Oceans was taught by student Logan Rooper in the spring of 2013. The class built a remotely operated vehicle and sent it to the ocean just off the coast of Newport, Oregon. The class collected data about the levels of Cesium-137 in the water, while also collecting video, ph, temperature, and sound data.

XV: Astronomy (Exploratory Ventures)

March 2014 – September 2014

Exploratory Ventures is a class taught at Riverdale High School by high school students. This project-based course allows students to learn a wide variety of skills and immediately apply them.
